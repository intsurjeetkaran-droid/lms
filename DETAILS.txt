HOW IT WORKS
==================================================

FOR CUSTOMERS:
1. Sign up and create an account
2. Find nearby laundry service providers on a map
3. Select items you want to wash (shirts, pants, etc.)
4. Choose your pickup location
5. Submit your order
6. Provider reviews and gives you final price
7. Pay online (UPI) or cash on delivery
8. Track your order status
9. Receive clean clothes
10. Rate and review the service

FOR SERVICE PROVIDERS:
1. Register your laundry business
2. Set up your shop location and service area
3. Add items you wash with prices (shirt ₹20, jeans ₹30, etc.)
4. Set your payment details (UPI ID, QR code)
5. Receive orders from nearby customers
6. Review orders and set prices for special items
7. Pick up clothes from customer
8. Wash, dry, and iron
9. Deliver back to customer
10. Receive payment

FOR ADMINISTRATORS:
1. Monitor all activities on the platform
2. Approve new service providers
3. Handle customer complaints
4. Block bad users if needed
5. View business statistics and revenue

PRICING STRUCTURE
==================================================

The system calculates price as:

ITEM COSTS:
- Shirt: ₹20 (example)
- Jeans: ₹30 (example)
- Custom items: Provider sets price

ADDITIONAL CHARGES:
- Pickup charge: Set by provider
- Delivery charge: Set by provider
- Distance charge: ₹5 per km (example)
- Extra charges: For special requests

TOTAL = Items + Pickup + Delivery + Distance + Extra

Example:
- 2 Shirts (₹20 each) = ₹40
- 1 Jeans = ₹30
- Pickup = ₹20
- Delivery = ₹20
- Distance (3 km × ₹5) = ₹15
- TOTAL = ₹125

==================================================
ORDER LIFECYCLE
==================================================

STEP 1: ORDER CREATION
- Customer selects items and location
- System calculates base price
- Order sent to provider

STEP 2: PROVIDER REVIEW
- Provider sees order details
- Sets prices for custom items
- Adds any extra charges
- Confirms final price

STEP 3: CUSTOMER CONFIRMATION
- Customer sees final price
- Chooses payment method
- Confirms order

STEP 4: PICKUP
- Provider navigates to customer location
- Collects clothes
- Updates status to "Picked Up"

STEP 5: PROCESSING
- Provider washes, dries, irons
- Updates status to "Processing"

STEP 6: DELIVERY
- Provider delivers clean clothes
- Updates status to "Delivered"
- Collects payment if COD

STEP 7: COMPLETION
- Customer confirms receipt
- Gives rating and review
- Order marked "Completed"

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
==================================================

Q: How do I find providers near me?
A: System automatically shows providers within their service radius from your location.

Q: Can I cancel an order?
A: Yes, before pickup. After pickup, contact provider or admin.

Q: What if clothes are damaged?
A: Raise a complaint immediately with details. Admin will investigate.

Q: How do I change my prices as a provider?
A: Go to "Manage Garments" and update prices anytime.

Q: Is my payment information safe?
A: Yes, we don't store payment details. UPI payments go through official apps.

Q: Can I serve multiple areas as a provider?
A: Yes, set your service radius (up to 20 km).

Q: What if provider doesn't show up?
A: Contact admin immediately. We'll resolve and may penalize provider.

Q: How do I become a provider?
A: Register, complete profile, wait for admin approval, start receiving orders.
   (If using test data, provider accounts are pre-approved)

Q: Can I test the system without registering?
A: Yes! If the seeder was run, use test accounts from credentials.txt file.
