==================================================
WHAT IS THIS SYSTEM?
==================================================

A complete online laundry management platform that connects:

  CUSTOMERS        — people who need laundry done
  SERVICE PROVIDERS — laundry businesses offering the service
  ADMINISTRATORS   — platform managers

Think of it like Uber, but for laundry.

==================================================
HOW IT WORKS
==================================================

FOR CUSTOMERS:
  1. Register an account (5-step process)
  2. Find nearby laundry providers on a map
  3. Select garments to wash (shirt, jeans, etc.)
  4. Add custom items if needed — you'll see the provider's
     allowed price range as a hint
  5. Set your pickup location via GPS
  6. Submit the order
  7. Provider reviews and confirms the final price
  8. Pay via UPI or cash on delivery
  9. Track your order status
 10. Receive clean clothes, then rate the service

FOR SERVICE PROVIDERS:
  1. Register your business (9-step process with GPS location)
  2. Wait for admin approval
  3. Set up your shop profile and service area
  4. Add garments with prices to your catalog
  5. Set your custom item price range (default ₹20–₹100)
     — this range is shown to customers as a hint
     — garment prices outside your range are blocked
  6. Configure payment details (UPI ID, QR code, charges)
  7. Receive and review orders from nearby customers
  8. Set prices for any custom items in the order
  9. Pick up, wash, and deliver clothes
 10. Receive payment

FOR ADMINISTRATORS:
  1. Approve new service providers
  2. Monitor all orders and users
  3. Block users who violate platform rules
  4. Handle customer complaints
  5. View revenue and platform statistics

==================================================
CUSTOM ITEM PRICE RANGE
==================================================

Each provider sets their own price range for custom items.

DEFAULT RANGE: ₹20 minimum — ₹100 maximum

Providers can change this range anytime from the
"Manage Garments" page. The new range takes effect
immediately for all future garment saves.

When a customer adds a custom item to an order, they
see a hint showing that provider's specific range, so
they know what price to expect.

If a provider tries to save a garment price outside
their own range, the system blocks it with an error.

==================================================
PRICING STRUCTURE
==================================================

ITEM COSTS:
  Standard garments — price set by provider
  Custom items      — price set by provider during order review,
                      within their defined price range

ADDITIONAL CHARGES (set by provider):
  Pickup charge
  Delivery charge
  Distance charge (per km)
  Extra charges for special requests

TOTAL = Items + Pickup + Delivery + Distance + Extra

Example:
  2 Shirts (₹20 each)     = ₹40
  1 Jeans                 = ₹30
  Pickup charge           = ₹20
  Delivery charge         = ₹20
  Distance (3 km × ₹5)   = ₹15
  TOTAL                   = ₹125

==================================================
ORDER LIFECYCLE
==================================================

STEP 1 — ORDER CREATION
  Customer selects items and pickup location.
  Order sent to provider with status: awaiting_review

STEP 2 — PROVIDER REVIEW
  Provider sees order details.
  Sets prices for any custom items.
  Adds extra charges if needed.
  Confirms final price. Status: confirmed

STEP 3 — CUSTOMER CONFIRMATION
  Customer sees final price.
  Chooses payment method (UPI or COD).
  Confirms order.

STEP 4 — PICKUP
  Provider collects clothes from customer.
  Status: picked_up

STEP 5 — PROCESSING
  Provider washes, dries, irons.
  Status: processing

STEP 6 — DELIVERY
  Provider delivers clean clothes.
  Collects payment if COD.
  Status: delivered

STEP 7 — COMPLETION
  Customer confirms receipt.
  Leaves rating and review.
  Status: completed

==================================================
PAYMENT OPTIONS
==================================================

UPI — Customer pays via Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.
      Enters transaction ID to confirm payment.

COD — Cash collected by provider on delivery.

No credit card storage. No payment data stored on platform.

==================================================
COMPLAINT RESOLUTION
==================================================

1. Customer raises a complaint from the order page
2. Selects complaint type:
     Quality Issue / Delivery Issue / Payment Issue /
     Behavior Issue / Other
3. Describes the problem
4. Admin reviews and contacts both parties
5. Issue resolved and complaint marked as resolved

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
==================================================

Q: How do I find providers near me?
A: The system automatically shows providers within their
   service radius from your GPS location.

Q: What is the custom item price range?
A: Each provider sets their own range (default ₹20–₹100).
   You'll see it as a hint when adding custom items.
   The provider sets the actual price during order review.

Q: Can I cancel an order?
A: Yes, before pickup. After pickup, contact the provider
   or raise a complaint with admin.

Q: What if my clothes are damaged?
A: Raise a complaint immediately with details.
   Admin will investigate and resolve fairly.

Q: How do I update my prices as a provider?
A: Go to "Manage Garments" and edit prices anytime.
   You can also update your custom item price range there.

Q: Is my payment information safe?
A: Yes. No payment details are stored on the platform.
   UPI payments go through official apps.

Q: How do I become a provider?
A: Register, complete your profile, and wait for admin
   approval. Once approved, you can start receiving orders.

Q: Can I test without registering?
A: Yes. If the seeder was run, use test accounts from
   credentials.txt. Example: customer1@test.com / 123456
